Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), bank transfer, and card payments. All payments are processed securely.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

The Free plan lets you try the platform with no commitment. Paid plans start billing immediately but you can cancel anytime before the next cycle.

What happens to my listings if I cancel?

If you cancel a paid plan, your account reverts to the Free tier. Listings beyond the free limit will be paused (not deleted) until you reactivate or reduce your active listings.
