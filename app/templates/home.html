FAQ

Answers to questions about browsing, listing, and using the platform.

How do I search for listings?

Use the search bar on the Listings page to filter by location, price, and property type. You can narrow results by area in Kampala or browse all available properties. No account required to search.

Are all agents verified?

Yes. Every agent on Rentals Directory goes through verification before listing properties. We check credentials and background to ensure you're dealing with legitimate professionals.

How do I contact an agent?

Click on any listing to see the agent's details. You can message them directly through the platform or use the contact information provided. Response times are usually quick.

Can I post my own listing?

Yes. Create an account and access the Agent Dashboard to post properties. You'll need to provide property details, photos, and pricing. Listings go live after verification.

Is there a cost to list?

Subscription plans are available for agents who want premium features and visibility. Basic listings are free. Check the pricing page for current plans and details.

How do I know listings are real?

All listings are posted by verified agents. We remove inactive listings and flag suspicious activity. If something seems off, report it and we'll investigate.
